About AI Camera Emotion Recognition System

General Purpose

An artificial intelligence system that uses a camera to analyze and understand human emotions through facial expression analysis. The system focuses on reading basic emotions (such as happiness, sadness, anger), dual emotions (such as anxiety mixed with anger), and providing special support for people with psychological disorders such as autism.

Key Features

  • Basic Emotion Analysis: Identifying basic emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, fear, disgust, surprise, and neutrality.
  • Dual Emotion Analysis: Recognition of complex emotional states such as anxiety mixed with anger or sadness mixed with hesitation.
  • Autism Support: Providing a specialized tool for understanding human emotions for people with autism spectrum disorder.
  • Camera Integration: Capturing images or videos directly from the camera and analyzing them in real-time.
  • Accurate Reports: Creating analytical reports about detected emotions that can be used in scientific research or medical applications.

Technical Components

  • OpenCV: For processing video and images from the camera.
  • TensorFlow / Keras: For building and training deep learning models.
  • NumPy and Matplotlib: For data processing and visual display of results.
  • Flask: For creating an interactive web user interface.

Potential Applications

  • Mental Health: Assisting doctors in diagnosing disorders such as depression or anxiety.
  • Education: Improving understanding of student emotional responses.
  • Social Robots: Improving interaction between robots and humans.
  • Autism: Providing an educational tool to understand human emotions.
